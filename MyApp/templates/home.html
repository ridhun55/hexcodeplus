Welcome To hexcodeplus

Hexcodeplus Technologies is an innovative startup company that supplying a turn-key solution in electronics industry based upon the latest technology and utilizing Web-based applications in the field of circuit design and PCB printing.

We provide the online service for students, hobbyist, small scale entrepreneurs, an online solution that help them to create custom printed circuit boards with low cost and without any knowledge about the circuit board designing or printing. Which means they can simply print from there handmade schematic circuit diagram with us and also we provide various standardized circuits for the customers for reference

Our services are: PCB fabrication, prototyping, custom product design, components distribution, Robotics and electronic tutorials, Project assistance, Online PCB printing, STEM
